# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Always verify the sender before clicking on any email or message link.
- Do not share personal information like passwords or OTPs with anyone.
- Check website URLs carefully before entering login details.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests or collect requests.
- Always double-check the receiver's UPI ID before sending money.
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches.
- Report suspicious activities immediately.
