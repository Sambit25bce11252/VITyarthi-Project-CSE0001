# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts.
- Verify information before sharing it online.
- Use privacy settings to control who can view your content.
- Think before posting anything that could affect your reputation.
- Engage positively and respectfully with others.
- Keep your profiles updated with accurate and appropriate information.
- Use social media to learn, network, and build useful connections.

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in online arguments or toxic discussions.
- Avoid accepting requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
- Do not spread misinformation or unverified news.
- Avoid oversharing daily activities or location details.
- Do not use social media irresponsibly during academic or professional work.
