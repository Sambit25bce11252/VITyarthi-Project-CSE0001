# Task 2: Reflection Notes

For this task, I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to build a strong digital presence. Each platform serves a different purpose in the academic and professional journey. 

GitHub is mainly used to showcase projects, maintain code repositories, and collaborate with others. It allows me to track my progress and demonstrate my skills in programming. 

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where I can connect with peers, seniors, and professionals, as well as explore internship and career opportunities.

Kaggle is focused on data science and provides a platform to practice coding, work with datasets, and learn from real-world problems.

Working on this task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional and well-organized online profile. Over the next four years, I plan to regularly update my GitHub with new projects and improvements, enhance my LinkedIn profile by adding skills, certifications, and achievements, and actively participate in Kaggle to improve my problem-solving abilities. These platforms will play an important role in helping me prepare for internships and future career opportunities.
